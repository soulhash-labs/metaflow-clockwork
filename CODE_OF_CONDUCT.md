# Code of Conduct

## Summary

MetaFlow Clockwork expects respectful, technically focused collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and precise
- assume good faith
- critique ideas and code, not people
- keep discussion relevant to the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- doxxing or sharing private information
- repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, pull requests, or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct, and may restrict participation when needed to keep the project usable.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues through the repository maintainers or the repository host's reporting path. Do not use public issues for personal-safety reports.
